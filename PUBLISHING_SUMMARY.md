### 2. Upload to Chrome Web Store

1. **Go to**: [Chrome Web Store Developer Dashboard](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/devconsole/)

2. **Register** (if not already):
   - Pay one-time $5 fee
   - Accept Developer Agreement

3. **Create New Item**:
   - Click "New Item"
   - Upload `pramana-context-v2.2.2.zip`

4. **Fill Store Listing**:
   - **Name**: Pramana Context - Tab Intent Tracker
   - **Summary** (132 chars):
     ```
     Remember why you opened each tab. Track intent, add notes, and organize your research with tags. Privacy-first, locally stored.
     ```
   - **Description**: See PUBLISHING_GUIDE.md for full description
   - **Category**: Productivity
   - **Language**: English
   - **Icon**: Upload `assets/icons/icon128.png`
   - **Screenshots**: Upload 1-5 screenshots (1280x800)

5. **Privacy**:
   - **Single Purpose**:
     ```
     This extension helps users remember why they opened each browser tab by allowing them to add intent notes and tags, track visit history, and organize contexts by domain.
     ```
   - **Permissions Justification**:
     - tabs: Required to read page URLs and titles
     - storage: Required to save data locally
   - **Privacy Policy**: Upload PRIVACY_POLICY.md or provide URL

6. **Submit**: Click "Submit for Review"
   - Review time: 1-3 business days
   - You'll receive email notification
