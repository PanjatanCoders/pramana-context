# Privacy Policy for Pramana Context

**Last Updated**: December 29, 2025

## Overview

Pramana Context ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect
The Extension collects and stores the following information **locally on your device only**:

- **Page URLs**: Web addresses you visit (when auto-save is enabled or when you manually save)
- **Page Titles**: The title of each webpage
- **Intent Notes**: Your personal notes about why you opened each page
- **Visit Metadata**: Visit counts, timestamps, and time spent on pages
- **User Preferences**: Your settings (auto-save toggle, group view preference)

### What We DO NOT Collect
- We do NOT send any data to external servers
- We do NOT track your browsing history beyond what you choose to save
- We do NOT collect personal information
- We do NOT use analytics, telemetry, or tracking tools
- We do NOT share or sell your data to anyone

## How We Use Your Data

All data collected by the Extension is:
- **Stored locally** using Chrome's storage API on your device
- **Never transmitted** to any external server or third party
- **Used exclusively** to provide the Extension's functionality
- **Fully controlled by you** - you can view, edit, export, or delete it at any time

## Data Storage

- All data is stored using Chrome's local storage API (`chrome.storage.local`)
- Data persists on your device until you manually delete it or uninstall the Extension
- The Extension automatically cleans up resolved contexts older than 30 days

## Data Sharing

**We do not share your data with anyone.** Period.

Your data never leaves your device. There are no servers, no third-party services, no analytics platforms.

## User Control

You have complete control over your data:

- **View**: Access all your saved contexts through the search interface
- **Edit**: Modify intent notes and context status at any time
- **Export**: Download all your data in JSON format
- **Delete**: Remove individual contexts or bulk delete multiple items
- **Disable**: Turn off auto-save to manually control what gets tracked

## Permissions

The Extension requires the following Chrome permissions:

### tabs
- **Why**: To identify which pages you're visiting
- **Usage**: Read page URLs and titles for saving contexts
- **Limitation**: Only accessed when you visit HTTP/HTTPS pages

### storage
- **Why**: To save your context data locally on your device
- **Usage**: Store and retrieve your saved pages, intent notes, and preferences
- **Limitation**: Data never leaves your device

## Data Retention

- Data is retained indefinitely on your device until you delete it
- Resolved contexts older than 30 days are automatically deleted
- Uninstalling the Extension removes all stored data from your device

## Children's Privacy

The Extension does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The Extension is not directed at children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.

## Data Security

- All data is stored using Chrome's secure storage API
- No data transmission means no risk of data breaches during transfer
- Your data security depends on your device's security

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access all your data through the Extension interface
- Export your data in JSON format
- Delete any or all of your data at any time
- Disable the Extension's data collection features

## Uninstalling the Extension

When you uninstall Pramana Context:
- All locally stored data is automatically removed by Chrome
- No data remains on any external servers (because we don't use any)

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or the Extension's data practices, please:
- Open an issue on our GitHub repository
- Contact the developer through the Chrome Web Store listing

## Compliance

This Extension complies with:
- Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) principles

## Open Source

Pramana Context is open source. You can review the complete source code to verify our privacy practices.

---

**In Summary**: Pramana Context stores everything locally on your device. We don't collect, transmit, share, or sell your data. You have complete control over your information.
